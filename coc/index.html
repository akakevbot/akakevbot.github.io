Why Puyallup.

Only a short distance from SeaTac airport and caught between the Fort Lewis Military Base and Seattle, Puyallup is an ideal location for any business given it's highly sought after work force in a wide variety of fields. It also benefits from nearby tourist attractions, attracting nearly 2.5 million visitors each year to the Washington State Fair and other events.
