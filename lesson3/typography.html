Headers and CTA's:

Bebas Neue will be used for headings. This all-caps font will provide a clear distinction between the paragraph text and the heading text or calls to action. While it would be cumbersome to read multiple lines of this font, its weight lends impact to the headings and makes them stand out.

Paragraphs and other font:

Poppins will be used for paragraphs and other text. This sans serif font is much easier to read than Babas Neue but carries many similar qualities. It will help link the content and the headings together, while still maintaining readability.
