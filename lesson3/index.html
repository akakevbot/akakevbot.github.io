Title:

Weather Me Not

Purpose:

There are a lot of weather websites and they all basically have the same information. Wheather me not provides weather but in the context of how different the current day is vs. Rather than surfacing all the same weather information that you will get from any other weather app, Weather Me Not will only display unique information if the day differs from the average for your location and the time of year.

The goal is to simplify checking the weather to a simple Yes/No. If it is summer and I expect most days to be sunny and warm, weather me not will confirm if days fit the description. Only if they don't will it surface additional informaiton and then only the information which is different. For example, if it will still be warm, but will rain. Weather me not will only surface that it will rain.
